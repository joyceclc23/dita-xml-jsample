1. Introduction

Lattice Avant™ FPGA devices are architected and designed for low power consumption. Power supply levels, process technology, device configuration, circuit topologies, and integration methods have all been chosen with the goal of providing the lowest possible power while satisfying performance needs. Low power techniques are either designed into the FPGA itself, or implemented automatically without user input needed. This technical note serves as a usage guide for managing and determining the power consumption of Avant devices. The document details the conceptual and functional description along with a guide to utilize the power saving functions. Analyzing power consumption in your design using the Lattice Radiant® software Power Calculator tool is also described.
